Lismova — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version

Lismova collects nothing. There is no account, no sign-up, no ads, no analytics or tracking, and no servers. The app works fully offline and everything you do stays on your device.

Information the app collects

None. Lismova does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. It does not ask you to create an account and does not require an internet connection to function.

Data stored on your device

Your learning progress — completed lessons, XP, streak, achievements and settings — is saved locally on your device so the app can remember where you left off. This information never leaves your device, is not accessible to me, and is removed if you delete the app.

Advertising, analytics & third parties

Lismova contains no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. The app makes no network requests of its own. Standard platform services provided by Apple or Google (for example, app distribution and crash reporting you may have enabled at the operating-system level) are governed by those companies' own privacy policies.

Children

Lismova is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this policy will be updated here with a new "last updated" date before those changes take effect.
