Side Project

Lismova Coming soon

Learn Belarusian — pixel-art lessons & flashcards

A Duolingo-style app for learning Belarusian — one of Europe's most underserved languages, which the big apps simply don't teach. The name blends ліс (fox) and мова (language), with a pixel-art fox as the mascot. Built in React Native with a hand-styled retro aesthetic: bite-sized lessons, a swipeable flashcard deck, and full verb conjugations — all fully offline, no account, no ads.

I'm building it solo, with content verified by a native speaker. The pixel art is rendered with React Native Skia for crisp upscaling on high-DPI screens.
